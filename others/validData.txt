Valid data:

 -First Name must be 3 or more characters long.

 -Last name must be 3 to 20 characters long.

 -Phone Number must be 10 digit and only numbers are allowed.

 -Password and Confirm Password must match with each other.

 -Password must be at least 8 characters long and must have 1 special character, 1 capital 1, numeric 1 small.

 -Email must contain characters before the '@' symbol and end with either '@gmail.com' or '@yahoo.com'.
